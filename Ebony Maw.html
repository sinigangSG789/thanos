About Ebony Maw

Ebony Maw: The Mastermind of the Black Order

Ebony Maw is one of the most intelligent and unsettling villains in the Marvel Universe. As a high-ranking member of the Black Order, he serves as a loyal enforcer and advisor to Thanos, carrying out his mission with precision, strategy, and terrifying calm.

Who is Ebony Maw?

Ebony Maw is not a typical warrior. Unlike other members of the Black Order who rely on brute strength, he is a master manipulator and tactician. He prefers to control situations from a distance, using intelligence and psychological influence rather than physical force.

In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Ebony Maw appears in Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame, where he plays a key role in capturing powerful heroes and advancing Thanos' plan to collect the Infinity Stones.
