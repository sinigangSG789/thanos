About Thanos

Thanos is one of the most powerful and complex villains in the Marvel Universe. Known as the “Mad Titan,” he is a cosmic warlord from the planet Titan, driven by a twisted sense of balance and destiny. Thanos possesses immense strength, intelligence, and mastery of advanced technology, making him a nearly unstoppable force even without enhancements. His most iconic goal is collecting the six Infinity Stones—Power, Space, Reality, Mind, Time, and Soul—which grant him god-like abilities when combined in the Infinity Gauntlet. With these, he seeks to eliminate half of all life in the universe, believing it will restore balance and prevent overpopulation and suffering. Despite his ruthless actions, Thanos is often portrayed as a philosophical character, convinced that his destructive mission is necessary for the greater good. This mix of intelligence, conviction, and overwhelming power makes him one of Marvel's most memorable and feared antagonists.
